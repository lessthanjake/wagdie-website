## Placeholder Strategy

Until actual assets are added, you can:
1. Use placeholder services like `https://via.placeholder.com/800x600`
2. Create simple colored rectangles with text
3. Use temporary stock images

## Asset Requirements

- **Images**: PNG or JPG format, optimized for web
- **Videos**: MP4 format, H.264 codec, max 1080p recommended
- **Logo**: Transparent PNG preferred
- **OpenGraph**: Exactly 1200x630px for best social sharing
- **Cards**: Recommended 800x600px or 16:9 aspect ratio
